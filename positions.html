PhD Topics
We actively look for talented PhD students; currently, the following topics are available:

  • Multi-layered Auxetic-core Energy-absorbing Composites

    Micromechanical models are considered in conjunction with computational techniques and experimental validation. Developing new continuum mechanical models are also of interest.

  • Hygro-thermal Modelling of CLT Timber Structures

    Micromechanical models are considered in conjunction with computational techniques and experimental validation. Developing new continuum mechanical models are also of interest.

  • Modelling Fibre-reinforced Composites

    Micromechanical models are considered in conjunction with computational techniques and experimental validation. Developing new continuum mechanical models are also of interest.

PhD Applications
In order to apply, please send an email (limited to 200 words max) titled "PhD application (auxeticslab)"; in addition, please provide the following items:
  • CV,
  • transcripts (Bachelor's and Master's),
  • the topic of interest, and
  • one best publication.
