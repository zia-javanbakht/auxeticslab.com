About Auxetics Lab

We are interested in developing novel high-performance composite and auxetic materials. In this context, we try to adopt and extend computational approaches to simulate the material behaviour and improve our understanding.

Research Focus

We are interested in developing novel high-performance composite and auxetic materials. In this context, we try to adopt and extend computational approaches to simulate the material behaviour and improve our understanding. Our current focus is improving the energy-absorbing characteristics of multi-layer structures.
