Research Focus

We aim to leverage the state-of-the-art technology in material development and produce smart products that enhance the quality of life. We are interested in developing novel high-performance composite and auxetic materials. We try to adopt and extend computational approaches to simulate the material behaviour and improve our understanding about solids. Our current focus is improving the energy-absorbing characteristics of multi-layer structures.
